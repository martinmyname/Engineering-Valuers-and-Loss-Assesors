OBJECTIVES OF THE ASSOCIATION

a)To act as means of communication between members and the public plus the industrial sectors.

b)Making business more effective, safe, creative, scientific, & for securing and justfying the confidence of the public through competence trusworthness and ethical standards of members and ofall those involved in valuation and assesment work .

c)Promoting personal and friendly relationship between members of the association by holding conferences, seminars, meetings and public for the discussion of professional affairs.

d)To lend or advance money to mebers and other persons on securities of all description wether real or personal and to grant loans upon mortgage of any land buildings and there determents.

e)Promoting and fostering education and in the training of insurance in general and employees liability fideliyty guarantee, third party, burglary and theft, fire, life, marine and vehicle in particular including mortgage or other investment insurance.
